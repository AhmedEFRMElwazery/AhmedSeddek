Ahmed Seddek, born in Cairo in 1987, has been working as a Senior Consultant at the Ministry of Planning and Economic Development (MEPD) in Egypt since 2019. Prior to his current post, he worked as a Quality Managder at Majid Al Futtaim Group on a country-wide level in Egypt, and other companies.

Mr. Seddek received his Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Paris ESLSCA Business School in 2021. His MSc thesis title is "Assembling Egypt Government Excellence Award An Exploratory Approach to Analyze Critical Success Factors for Implementing Excellence Model in Public Sector". He also hold qualifications in Business Administration and Management from the American University in Cairo (AUC), and in Veternary Medicine (BSc) from Cairo University, respectivetly. He was nominated for the Presidential Leadership Program (PLP.EG) in 2018, and later on for the Government Leadership Program in 2020, that was co-supervised and co-delivered by Cairo University, Missuori State University and the National Institute of Management. He is fluend in Arabic and English. For further details, please feel free to download his CV.
